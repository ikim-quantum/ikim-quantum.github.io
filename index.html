Isaac H. Kim

Assistant Professor, UC Davis, Dept. of Computer Science

I work in the field of Quantum Information and Quantum Computation. My research goal is to understand fundamental aspects of quantum entanglement that appear in nature. I have a fairly broad interest in anything quantum. I have worked on the following topics.

  • Topological Order
  • Quantum Error Correction
  • Quantum Algorithms
  • Black-hole information paradox
  • Matrix Analysis

These days, I am primarily focused on these topics.

  • Entanglement bootstrap program
  • Practical aspects of quantum error correction
  • Tensor networks on near-term quantum computers
