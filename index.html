Isaac H. Kim


Assistant Professor of Computer Science at UC Davis


I am a theorist working on quantum computing and quantum many-body physics. My goal is to solve interacting quantum many-body systems from first principles, either analytically or numerically.


Currently, I am primarily working on the following topics.
  • Quantum algorithms
  • Quantum error correction
  • Entanglement bootstrap
I also have a side interest in a fascinating connection between quantum information and holographic duality. Papers
